Intelligent Discoveries

About

BioXplor is an AI platform for life science discovery. The in silico discovery platform is built on the intersection of big data, artificial intelligence, and high-performance computing. The data platform accelerates knowledge generation and novel predictions from structured and unstructured life science data. The platform is optimized for drug repurposing, experiment design, biomarker and drug target identification.
